🏦 Wealth tax $0/yr

Annual tax on net assets above $50M.

Elizabeth Warren's proposed 2% on wealth above $50M / 3% above $1B was estimated to raise ~$200B/yr. Proponents argue top wealth is largely self-perpetuating and lightly taxed. Critics cite capital flight risk and valuation difficulty for illiquid assets. Roosevelt Inst.

🛒 Value-added tax (VAT) $0/yr

Consumption tax applied at each production stage. Used by most developed nations.

A 10% VAT on the ~$18T US consumption base raises ~$950B/yr after exemptions. Andrew Yang's Freedom Dividend relied on a 10% VAT. Regressive on its own — but UBI fully offsets this for anyone below median income, making the combined policy progressive. Yang2020

🌾 Land value tax (LVT) $0/yr

Tax on the unimproved value of land — not buildings. Championed by Thomas Paine and Henry George.

Total US land value is ~$23 trillion. An LVT taxes location value — created by society and infrastructure, not the owner's effort. Economists across the spectrum consider it one of the most efficient taxes: it cannot be avoided by working less or moving capital. Paine proposed a version in 1797. Paine, SSA archive

☁️ Carbon dividend $0/yr

Fee on fossil fuel emissions returned as equal cash dividends to all citizens.

The Climate Leadership Council proposes $40–$100/ton, returning all proceeds as citizen dividends. Alaska's oil fund is the working model. Revenue declines over time as emissions fall — which is the point. CLC

📈 Financial transaction tax $0/yr

Small tax on stock, bond, and derivative trades. Primarily paid by high-frequency traders.

The CBO estimates a 0.1% rate raises ~$78B/yr. ~$100T in annual US financial transactions. Revenue is sensitive to behavioral change — volume falls as rates rise, which is also the policy intent. CBO

🤖 Automation levy $0/yr

Tax on companies replacing workers with automation. Proposed by Bill Gates.

Links the cause directly to the remedy: companies profiting from displacement pay for the safety net beneath displaced workers. Estimated ~$1.5T in automated labor substitution value annually and growing rapidly. Key challenge is defining and measuring "automated displacement." Quartz/Gates

💼 Higher top income tax rates $0/yr

Raise marginal rates on incomes above $400K. The US top rate was 91% in the 1950s.

Each point above the current 37% top rate raises ~$15–20B/yr. The top rate was 91% through 1963, 70% through 1980, 50% through 1986. Tax Policy Center
